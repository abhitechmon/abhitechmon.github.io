Our Process


  • Discovery
  • Strategy Development
  • Creative Concepting
  • Production & Excution
  • Analysis & Results

Discovery

The initial project discovery phase involves collection and organization of customer requirements, researching the target audience’s needs and risk assessments are taken into consideration to give effective and realistic price estimates.

Strategy Development

We believe in developing a sound strategy which is built upon rational thinking, prioritizing and developing excellent research skills. Strategy development encompasses a long term plan which will help us achieve our goals.

Creative Concepting

Creative concepting includes tapping into the audience's interests and needs and accordingly taking action by building up ideas that are distinctive, memorable and relevant. Our creative team comes up with multiple creative concepts and then concept tests them to zero down to that one idea which best fits with our client’s ideas.

Production & Execution

We realize that delivering a project without proper testing would lead to failure. Our development team ensures that all our projects are followed according to schedules. Along with that we continuously monitor our project to remove all errors and possible risks that can come along with it.

Ongoing Analysis & Results

Towards the end of our project, we perform a final analysis to eliminate redundancies, if any and check whether the project meets the demands of our clients. The end result would be the final working project which would ensure that our mission of ‘client satisfaction’ is achieved.
